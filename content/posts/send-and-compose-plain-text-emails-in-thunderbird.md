> *Originally published on https://nektro.mataroa.blog/*

## Send

Inbox > Write

Options > Delivery Format > Plain Text Only

## Compose

From the main menubar, Edit > Account Settings

Go to "Composition & Addressing"

Un-check "Compose messages in HTML format"
